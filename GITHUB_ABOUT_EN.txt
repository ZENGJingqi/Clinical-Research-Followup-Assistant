Short description:
A simple Django web application for patient tracking, clinical follow-up management, project enrollment, data export, and AI-assisted summaries.

Overview:
Clinical Research Followup Assistant is a web-based system for clinical research teams. It helps manage patients, treatments, follow-up visits, research projects, scales, attachments, and exportable data in one place. Optional AI features can also support faster patient summary writing.
